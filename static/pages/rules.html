Rules


Failing to follow these rules may result in a ban from using the site.
  • Accessing this site means you are responsible for the content of your posts, images, and the consequences of them.
  • 1. Do not post, request, or link to any illegal content; the staff will make judgments accordingly.
    Examples include Child Pornography (any pornography with someone who is 17 or younger, this includes lolicon, shotacon, etc), and Illegal copies of copyrighted content (this includes download links too). Violators will be permanently banned.
  • 2. Threats and flaming are not allowed. 
    Disruptive behaviors that prevent others from engaging in a thread are prohibited.
    Thread derailing is prohibited.
    Stalking, harassment, or posting of others' personal information are prohibited.
  • 3. No spamming of any kind, i.e. text, images, etc
  • 4. No malicious impersonation of other users.
  • 5. Respect each thread's topic. For example do not invade a thread where OP specifically asks for Person XYZ.
  • 6. Do not start flame wars. A debate is one thing, but constant bickering and arguing will be dealt with.
    These posts will be deleted, and the poster will be banned if this behavior continues
  • 7. Files that contain other (hidden) files are not allowed to be uploaded.
  • 8. When posting links don't do stuff like removing the first h from http in the URL or otherwise modify the link, so it has to be manually copied and restored before becoming a valid link. Also do not use pass-through pages or anonymizer services, link directly. The above also applies to links posted within images.
  • 9. Put what you are posting on the correct board, for example if you are spontaneously inviting someone to a fight,you should do that in /arena/.
  • Ban evasion will result in a doubling of the duration. Instead, wait and appeal it! Contact Caffeine on our Discord channel
  • The staff reserves the right to enforce these and all other rules and guidelines in the manner they see best for the site.
  • Unfounded Complaints about (rules, methods, staff or policies) on the threads it may end with a deleted post, The staff will be happy to help with any problems / questions you may have outside of the boards.
    Contact Caffeine on our Discord channel
    This website is a privilege. It is run and maintained by us in our spare time. We do it for YOUR continued enjoyment. So please show some respect and comprehension to the few of us in charge.
The following are not rules, but are discouraged. Repeated infractions will result in a ban, length of which to be determined by a moderator.
  • Posting full AI generated content is frowned upon
  • Excessive use Emoticons. I.e. Several rows of :-) or xD or other.
  • Political or Religious discussion is discouraged but more than welcome if it can be handled maturely
  • Don't create duplicate threads or threads similar to active ones who've yet to hit bump limit. If they've hit their bump limit however, this is fine.
  • No backseat moderating, if there's an issue that you feel cannot be dealt with in a civil manner report it to the site moderators.
  • Do not abuse or make false reports.
  • Do not engage other users in ways that could be deemed negative. To put it flat, don't be a dick.
  • Vulgarity and 'meme' speak is fine in moderation and if used in ways that don't negatively impact users.
    • Those that cannot follow these rules will be handled as appropriate for the case. Minor infractions will result in a warning.
    Major infractions or multiple warnings will at least result in a ban with the duration depending on the situation.

How Moderation Works

    The mods will do what they think is best. Here's some guidelines on what they're supposed to do:

  • Deal with arguments and derailing of threads.
  • Deal with anything that could get us in legal trouble.
  • Deal with people trolling or antagonizing other users.
  • Research reported posts, and determine an appropriate response.
  • Ban anyone who broke the above rules.
  • Coordinate with each other, and get opinions from the team.
